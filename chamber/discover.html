Know Our Promotions

Benefits of our stores

  • product quality: Selection of fresh and quality products: These supermarkets usually offer a wide variety of fresh products, such as fruits, vegetables, meats and bakery, with recognized quality standards.
  • Product variety: From basic products to gourmet, organic or specialized products, these supermarkets usually have a wide variety that meets different needs and tastes.
  • Comfort and convenience: These supermarkets are usually located in central or easily accessible areas, making it easier to shop without having to travel long distances.
  • Good customer service: They are known for offering more personalized customer service and quality service, especially on fresh produce.

Promotions in all supermarkets

  • Discounts on seasonal products: Many supermarkets offer discounts on typical Christmas products, such as panettone, nougat, butter, dried fruit and wine. These discounts can be applied to national and imported brands.
  • Offers on Christmas combos Description: Supermarkets create Christmas combos that include various products related to the celebration, such as Christmas chickens, nougat, wines, fruits and decorations at a reduced price.
  • Discounts on purchases for high amounts: Special discounts or coupons are offered for those who spend a certain amount in the store. This is a common strategy to encourage larger purchases during the holiday season.
  • "Get one and the second for half price" promotions: During the holidays, supermarkets often offer "buy one, get one half price" deals on popular products such as alcoholic beverages, snacks, and cleaning products.
